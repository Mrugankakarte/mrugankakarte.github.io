About Me

Graduate student at Columbia University, New York pursuing M.S in Data Science. Completed my B.Tech in Production Engineering at Vishwakarma Institute of Technology, Pune. I am interested in Reinforcement learning and its applications. I look forward to use my Data Science knowledge to solve problems in manufacturing industry.

During my under-graduation, I worked as Data Science intern at Ellicium Solutions Pvt. Ltd from Jan 2018- May 2018. I was a part of team and worked on data cleaning and model testing for a customer retention problem. Implemented a sub-module to gather data for real time analytics.

I am a big fan of koenigsegg Automobiles, especially the new Jesko model. I also love to play computer games and play tennis.
